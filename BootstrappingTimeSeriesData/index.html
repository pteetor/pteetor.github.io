CSP2014 - Bootstrapping Time Series Data

These are the materials for my presentation at the Conference on Statistical Practice in February 2014. The Papers subdirectory contains several papers related to bootstrapping time series data, some of which I drew upon for the talk.

Note: The R code available here is not production quality code. It should be taken as my working notes for the presentation and not necessarily suitable for your particular purposes.

  • Bootstrapping Time Series Data - The presentation slides [PDF, 6.4MB]
  • script.R - R script to perform examples used in the presentation
  • stockMarketRandomWalk.R - An example of bootstrapping stock market data
  • openImage.R - Utility function for generating presentation's images
  • Papers - Copies of research papers
